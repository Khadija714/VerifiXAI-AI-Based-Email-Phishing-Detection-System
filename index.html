How It Works

1. Text Preprocessing: Email content is cleaned, tokenized, and normalized using NLTK.

2. Feature Extraction: TF-IDF vectorization converts text into numerical features.

3. ML Classification: Random Forest algorithm classifies emails as safe or phishing with 92%+ accuracy.

4. Explainable AI: SHAP technology identifies which words contributed most to the decision.
